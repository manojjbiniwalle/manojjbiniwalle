About us

Biniwalle Associates offers unparalleled legal expertise across a comprehensive range of practice areas that are critical to our clients. The team possesses well-proven expertise in handling complex matters and is constantly aiming at optimum results operating on timelines. Biniwalle Associates team also specializes in in-depth legal research, legal assistance drafting, will writing or vetting of various types of documents.

The firm advises and practices in all aspects of various types of disputes resolution, divorce, family laws and the implication of criminal laws. The firm comprises professionals viz. advocates, specific consultants with experience that gives us practical legal solutions according to clients' needs.

The firm also practices and provides advice on matrimonial, family, dowry cases, criminal cases, cyber-crime, will writing, divorce, domestic violence, maintenance issues litigation arising Indore court jurisdiction and surrounding areas. Biniwalle Associates specializes in advising and implication of criminal laws. We have a track record of handling significant, high profile issues including criminal matters, matrimonial cases and cases pertaining to cyber-crime.

We can call upon a network of trusted counsel in various parts of India with whom we have built excellent working relationships. Through the course of our practice we had proudly added individuals, employees, companies, entrepreneurs.
